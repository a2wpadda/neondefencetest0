HOW TO PLAY

1. Kinetic Interception

Your core is surrounded by an orbiting kinetic shield. Tap the screen or hit SPACE to reverse its rotation. Smash incoming threats before they hit your core!

2. Hardware Upgrades

Destroying enemies yields Flux. Spend this between waves to extend your shield span, boost rotation speeds, or deploy perimeter Barricades.

3. Core Breach

If your core suffers lethal damage, you will be thrown back a wave. Guard it with your life.

Emits kinetic blasts that temporarily shove barricades outwards.

Accelerates exponentially. Becomes a blur right before core impact.

A test of memory. Flashes a colour for 1.15s, then splits into 5 identical octagons. Only the correct hue is real - striking a decoy causes severe core damage.

Mirror (Wave 42)

Projects a harmless holographic duplicate on the exact opposite side of the arena.

Magnet (Wave 45)

Exerts immense gravity. Physically drags your shield towards its intercept angle.

EMP-Leech (Wave 55)

High value target. If it breaches, it drains a massive 15% of your EMP charge.

The Nullifier (Wave 60)

Projects an anti-energy field that temporarily jams your offensive abilities.

Phalanx (Wave 65)

Directional front energy shield. Must be intercepted from the side or back, or crushed by barricades.

The Singularity (Wave 70)

A living black hole that pulls matter toward itself and spawns orbital deniers.

Glitch (Wave 75)

Reality corruption. It doesn't move smoothly, it teleports in sudden jagged bursts.

The Prism (Wave 80)

High-speed optical threat that refracts light into scattered projectile bursts.

Parasite (Wave 85)

The Corruptor (Wave 90)

The ultimate saboteur, scrambling your UI and reversing your rotational controls.

Doomsday (Wave 95)

Colossal threat. Rebounds exponentially faster. A single breach deletes 50% of your Health.

Omega Core (Wave 100)

The source of the Neon invasion. Absolute annihilation.
