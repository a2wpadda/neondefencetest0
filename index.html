HOW TO PLAY

1. Kinetic Interception

Your core is surrounded by an orbiting kinetic shield. Tap the screen or hit SPACE to reverse its rotation. Smash incoming threats before they hit your core!

2. Hardware Upgrades

Destroying enemies yields Flux. Spend this between waves to extend your shield span, boost rotation speeds, or deploy perimeter Barricades.

3. Core Breach

If your core suffers lethal damage, you will be thrown back a wave. Guard it with your life.

THREAT EXPANSION DOCUMENT

Part 1: Custom Bosses (Every 10 Waves)

Instead of generic polygons, every 10th wave will feature a named, unique boss with bespoke mechanics.

Wave 10 - "The Vanguard"
Appearance: A massive, heavily armoured square.
Mechanic: Moves slowly but periodically deploys a "frontal shield" facing the core. If you hit its shield, your kinetic shield bounces off and you take a massive shake penalty.
How to Defeat: You must wait for its frontal shield to temporarily retract (its "recharging" phase) and quickly strike the exposed hull with your kinetic shield. Deplete its health pool through these tight vulnerability windows.
Wave 20 - "The Swarm Queen"
(Already in game) - Dances erratically and rapidly hatches Stingers.
How to Defeat: You must weave through her relentless Stinger swarms and strike her main body with the kinetic shield. She rebounds when hit, requiring you to chase her down and land multiple strikes to chip away her massive health pool.
Wave 30 - "The Nullifier"
Appearance: A pulsing, dark blue hexagon.
Mechanic: Periodically fires an EMP pulse that temporarily disables your Barricades, forcing you to rely purely on your kinetic shields for 5 seconds.
How to Defeat: You can damage it with standard kinetic strikes. However, if you manage to strike it at the exact moment it is charging its EMP pulse, it interrupts the blast and reflects the energy inward, dealing massive bonus damage.
Wave 40 - "The Gemini"
Appearance: Two intertwined, crescent-shaped entities.
Mechanic: Two distinct bosses that orbit in opposite directions. They share a health pool, but if they cross paths and collide with each other, they unleash a massive shockwave of projectiles.
How to Defeat: Striking either crescent damages the shared health pool. To win efficiently, players with "Multi-Shields" can attempt to strike both entities simultaneously, which critically fractures their link and deals triple damage.
Wave 50 - "The Chronomancer"
Appearance: A glowing white, gear-like structure.
Mechanic: Manipulates the speed of the game. It will randomly cause your kinetic shield and all incoming projectiles to spin twice as fast or twice as slow, drastically messing with your reaction timing.
How to Defeat: It possesses temporal armour. It takes zero damage if struck while the game is sped up (it simply teleports away). You must survive the fast phases and only strike it when it shifts the game into "slow motion" to deal actual damage.

Part 2: New Enemy Types

New Rule - Enemy Obsolescence: To ensure the battlefield constantly evolves and doesn't become overly cluttered, standard enemies will "expire". If an enemy type appeared for the first time 10 waves ago or more, it will stop spawning entirely, making room for the newer, deadlier variants.

These new threats will be introduced at specific wave milestones to keep the late-game extremely challenging:

Wave 15 - "The Weaver" (Pink)
Mechanic: Instead of flying in a straight line towards the core, it travels in a zig-zag (sine wave) pattern. This makes it incredibly difficult to predict exactly where it will cross your shield orbit.
Wave 18 - "The Juggernaut" (Grey)
Mechanic: Extremely slow but boasts 4 HP. Unlike the Bouncer, it does not rebound when hit. It just eats the damage and keeps pushing forward, forcing you to hit it 4 times rapidly before it breaches the core.
Wave 22 - "The Cloaker" (Deep Red)
Mechanic: It stays completely visible, but the moment your kinetic shield or a barricade is about to hit it, it teleports 45 degrees to the left or right, forcing you to quickly change direction to catch it.
Wave 25 - "The Parasite" (Lime Green)
Mechanic: A fragile, fast enemy. If it breaches the core, it does not deal health damage. Instead, it steals 10% of your total banked Flux.
Wave 28 - "The Warden" (Cyan/White)
Mechanic: Moves slowly and projects an invulnerability tether to the two enemies closest to it. You cannot kill the tethered enemies until you smash the Warden first.
Wave 32 - "The Pulsar" (White)
Mechanic: Periodically emits a short-range blast as it travels. If a Barricade is caught in the blast radius, the Barricade is temporarily pushed outwards, creating a gap in your perimeter.
Wave 35 - "The Kamikaze" (Flashing Red/Yellow)
Mechanic: Starts incredibly slow at the edge of the screen, but its speed increases exponentially the closer it gets to the core, turning into a blur at the very last second.
Wave 38 - "The Trojan" (Hollow Orange)
Mechanic: Looks like a normal enemy, but upon destruction, it drops a fake "Flux" pickup. If the player's shield touches the fake Flux, it temporarily stuns the shield rotation.
Wave 42 - "The Mirror" (Silver)
Mechanic: Spawns a harmless, holographic duplicate of itself on the exact opposite side of the screen. They move in perfect sync, forcing the player to guess which one is the real threat that needs intercepting.
Wave 45 - "The Magnet" (Deep Purple)
Mechanic: Projects a gravitational field. When it gets close to the core, it physically pulls your kinetic shield towards its angle, forcing you to actively fight the pull to intercept other enemies.
Wave 48 - "The Shifter" (Teal)
Mechanic: Every few seconds, it instantly swaps positions with another random enemy currently on the screen.
Wave 55 - "The EMP-Leech" (Gold)
Mechanic: It moves moderately fast. Destroying it grants no Flux; instead, it drains 25% of your EMP charge bar when smashed. If it breaches the core, it drains the entire EMP bar.
Wave 65 - "The Phalanx" (Bronze)
Mechanic: Has a directional energy shield on its front that absorbs all kinetic impacts. It must be allowed to pass your Barricades or be hit from the side/behind by your orbiting shields as it crosses the line.
Wave 75 - "The Glitch" (Static/Flickering)
Mechanic: Moves in sudden, teleporting bursts rather than smooth motion. It stays perfectly still for a second, then instantly snaps forward 50 pixels, requiring rapid twitch reflexes to catch.
Wave 85 - "The Sapper" (Dark Green)
Mechanic: Ignores the core entirely. It locks onto your Barricades and attaches itself to them. If you don't crush it within 3 seconds, it permanently destroys the Barricade and spawns two Stingers from the wreckage.
Wave 95 - "The Doomsday" (Black with Red Aura)
Mechanic: Incredibly slow, but boasts a massive 15 HP. If this unit manages to touch the core, it deletes 50% of health unless its the player is at 1 hp where it deals the finishing blow.
